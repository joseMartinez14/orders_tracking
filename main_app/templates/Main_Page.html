Welcome to orders manager


This application will help your business to keep tracking for the orders you have whatever to order process is, and you will provide to your customers the transparency on the order they are always asking for, with a QR code to their order. You will show your customer what is the status or their order and all the steps their process need to go through in order to be complete, and you wonâ€™t be wasting your precious time replying to your customers if the order is ready or not or why is taking so long.

Let's see a usage example:

You are a company that creates personalized sweaters on demand.

  • Login / sign up
  • Register your company
  • Go to "Processes" and create a process in this example could be 'Build customer sweater'
  • In the same page, create all the steps this order requires, in this case.
    • Received 50% payment
    • Get needed materials
    • Elaborate the sweater
    • Received the other 50% payment
    • Send package through FedEx
  • Select "Create Order" and add the client lets say 'John Rodriguez' and select the process that the order will follow
  • Select "Orders Maintenance" you will see the new order and see the QR code and the link to display the order. Also, you can move to the next step from here
